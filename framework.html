Quantized Dimensional Ledger (QDL)

QDL treats dimensional analysis as a model-admissibility and validation layer: not merely “units consistent,” but “structurally allowed.”

1. Core dimensional structure

  • 3L + 2F basis: integer ledger representation.
  • Quantized Dimensional Cell (QDC): conserved closure target.
  • Ledger vectors: discrete composability.

2. Ledger closure as an admissibility rule

  • Closure replaces loose dimensional consistency.
  • Acts as a pre-phenomenological prediction filter.
  • Constrains operators, constants, and field content.
