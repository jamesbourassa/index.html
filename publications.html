Core QDL Manuscripts and Preprints

This page highlights the core manuscripts that define the Quantized Dimensional Ledger program: the unified framework, scalar–tensor extension, geometric underpinning of the Quantized Dimensional Cell, the ledger derivation of magnetic moments, metrology and constants, and the experimental protocol.

Several of these manuscripts are currently under review at journals in gravitation, mathematical physics, and metrology. The Zenodo records linked here serve as stable preprint and reference versions.
