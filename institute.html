The QDL Physics Institute

The QDL Physics Institute is an independent research organization dedicated to the development of the Quantized Dimensional Ledger (QDL), a unified dimensional-closure framework for theoretical physics and metrology.

Mission

The Institute advances foundational work on dimensional analysis, scalar–tensor gravitation, quantum field structure, and the geometry of physical constants. Research emphasizes transparency, reproducibility, formal rigor, and empirical testability through tabletop experiments.

Research Focus

Current projects include:

  • Unified dimensional structure of physical laws
  • Quantized Dimensional Cell (QDC) geometry
  • Ledger representations of Standard Model fields
  • Scalar–tensor gravitational formulations
  • Precision metrology and dimensional scaling tests
  • Experimental falsification pathways for QDL
