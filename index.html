Quantized Dimensional Ledger (QDL): Dimensional Closure as an Admissibility Framework

The Quantized Dimensional Ledger (QDL) is a structural admissibility framework that strengthens dimensional analysis into a dimensional-closure constraint on models—restricting admissible terms, couplings, and normalizations prior to phenomenological fitting. QDL functions as a prediction-filtering and validation layer across physics, cosmology, and measurement.
