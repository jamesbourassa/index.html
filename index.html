Program Snapshot

The QDL Physics Institute develops a ledger-first approach to physical theories, built on a two-basis dimensional system in length and frequency. The flagship unified framework paper introduces a closed ledger that simultaneously constrains the Standard Model sector, scalar–tensor gravitation, and the ontology of physical constants.

Subsequent works refine the geometry of the Quantized Dimensional Cell (QDC), derive precision predictions such as electron and muon g–2 factors, and map out a falsifiable experimental roadmap for torsion balances, NV-centers, cavity scaling, and metamaterials.
