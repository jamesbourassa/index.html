Experimental Program

The QDL Experimental Validation Protocol outlines four independent tabletop experiments that probe predictions derived from the Quantized Dimensional Ledger. These experiments are designed to be falsifiable, reproducible, and implementable using existing laboratory technologies.

1. Torsion-Balance Scaling

A precision mechanical test of QDL’s predicted dimensional scaling in weak-force torque systems.

2. NV-Center Frequency Shifts

Quantum-defect spectroscopy targeting ledger-predicted shifts in nitrogen–vacancy center spin resonances.

3. Cavity-Mode L–F Scaling

A direct laboratory demonstration of QDL’s length–frequency dimensional basis using tunable optical cavities.

4. Metamaterial Dispersion Collapse

Projected spectral signatures in engineered materials that track QDL ledger flows.
