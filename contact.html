Contact the QDL Physics Institute

For inquiries regarding research, editorial correspondence, collaboration, experimental proposals, clarification of QDL framework components, or public-communication matters, please use the information below. Responses are prioritized for scientific, editorial, and technical questions.

Subject line routing (recommended):
Use one of the following prefixes to help triage: [EDITOR/REFEREE], [COLLABORATION], [TECHNICAL QUESTION], [MEDIA].

When emailing, please include:

  • Your name and (if applicable) institutional affiliation
  • The topic area (e.g., gravitation, EFT/SMEFT, dimensional closure, constants, metrology, experimental tests)
  • Relevant DOI numbers (or direct paper titles) you’re referencing
  • Whether the inquiry is editorial, collaborative, or technical
  • Any specific question(s) you want answered (bullet points are ideal)
