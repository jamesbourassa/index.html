About the QDL Physics Institute

The QDL Physics Institute conducts theoretical and metrological research centered on the Quantized Dimensional Ledger (QDL) framework: a unified 3L + 2F dimensional basis, the conserved Quantized Dimensional Cell (QDC), and associated structures in gravitation, field theory, precision metrology, and experimental validation.

Founder

The Institute was founded by James D. Bourassa, an independent researcher based in Huntley, Illinois, USA. His work focuses on dimensional closure, ledger-based unification of physical theories, and falsifiable experimental strategies.
